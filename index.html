ABOUT ME

Whatever it is I'm doing, I want to be the best at it and I want it to improve people's lives. I am a true believer in the quote above along with John F Kennedy's quote "One person can make a difference, and everyone should try."

My coding career started out only a couple years ago, and I was completely self taught and motivated by an interest to build software that improves everyone's lives, or at least anyone with an internet connection. This originally stems from my volunteer experience and my company Good Influence. I started off learning HMTL and CSS and helped build very basic websites. I then grew my knowledge and expertise of Python, Java, and SQL in Coding Dojo's Full Stack Developer Bootcamp.

This past year my interest has strongly shifted towards Alexa and voice technology, as well as AWS and Cloud Computing. I have a strong desire to understand cyber security and software archeticturer in the cloud. I am currently working on building my Good Influence Alexa Skill, which provides motivational quotes, positive news stories, and recommendations for local volunteer opportunities.

Outside of work and coding, you can most likely find me playing a video game, playing with my dog, at the gym, or at the pool.

I've shared some of my projects that I built either as a student at Coding Dojo or in my spare time below.
